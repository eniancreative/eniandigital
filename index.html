How we work

1 Discovery and planning phase

Our first intent is to know the goals for creating your website. This phase is about information-gathering. It's critical to build the website according to the client's needs.

2 Create a strategy for SEO

We have the intention to increase the quality and quantity of website traffic to your website in organic search results.

3 Design of the website

Here we design how the website will look like. We have intention behind every design decision.

4 Development of the website

The website design is translated to actual code that makes the website work. We keep you informed on the progress by publishing website on daily basis at private domain name.

5 Launching the website

There are so many steps involved in launching a website. We make the process of launching your website much more efficient with considerable cost savings.

6 Maintenance of the website

Website maintenance is important for the long-term health and success of a website. A website needs new content, upkeep and maintenance.
